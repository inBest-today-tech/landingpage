INBEST NETWORK

A global decentralized multi-blockchain marketplace aiming to unlatch the future of investment and make the cryptocurrency market accessible to everyone.

How it works

Inbest Network is a global decentralized ecosystem built on top of the Blockchain technology. It coordinates blockchain co-signed orders and guarantees that any corresponding fees and gains are distributed fairly.

The Network connects Investors, Financial institutions, Individuals and Developers by implementing cosigned ERC20 Smart contracts.

The inbest Token -IBST- is the utility token used to exchange value within the network.

FOR INVESTORS

It solves the problem of accessibility,  ease of administration, transparency and yield management oriented to cryptocurrencies. A solution which investors can access through a number of intuitive mobile and web platforms.

FOR FINANCIAL PARTNERS

Ranging from passively managed crypto-currencies portfolios to automated trading strategies, Inbest Network facilitates a global decentralized marketplace where financial companies and institutions can make their products available for qualified investors.

FOR DEVELOPERS

Once the Inbest Network is deployed, it will provide and API for Developers to give free rein to their imagination and build their own platforms on top of it.

Proof of concept and real life applications

A curated user experience that implements IBST Tokens for users to invest in a suite of  passively managed diversified portfolios.

In order to demonstrate real life applications of the inbest Network we developed the inbest.today mobile app. This will also lead the way for entrepreneurs and developers to discover how they can leverage in numerous and innovative ways.

Inbest Token (IBST)

Inbest network is based on crypto-economic principles. IBST is the only crypto-currency that will be used for trading and investing in the network.
Token Name: INBEST
Ticker: IBST 
Token type: ERC-20
Token utility: Internal cryptocurrency to exchange value within the network between participants.

TOKEN SALE DETAILS

As part of our launch and funding strategy we announce the public crowdsale of Inbest Tokens

Distribution and supply

IBST Tokens supply will be fixed based on presale and crowdsale events.

100% of tokens will be distributed during the presale and crowdsale events.

10% of the Max Cap allocated for the Presale will be distributed to  Founders, Team, Advisors and Strategic partners over a 12 month vesting period, with a 6 months cliff. This means IBST tokens will not be immediately tradable, further aligning the Founders vision with execution of the long term goals of the project.

During the Presale, non-transferable blockchain-signed Certificates will be assigned to contributors. These Certificates will be immediately transformed to IBST tokens once the crowdsale event is over - PLEASE REVIEW PRESALE & CROWDSALE DETAILS BELOW -

Allocation of resources

The funds provided by the token sale are expected to be allocated as follow

60% of resources will be allocated to the development of the Inbest Network, which includes hiring and growing a development team, implementing business strategies, and other related expenses. 

16% of resources will be used for the legal, tax, and related corporate structure fees, and for potential liabilities. 

20% of resources will be part of the marketing budget to promote the Inbest Network. 

4% of resources will be kept for miscellaneous and other unforeseen expenses.
